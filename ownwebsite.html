My Story Synopses

Krishnudi Cherasaalalo Radha

In a village where an ancient temple owns 1000 acres of land cultivated by the villagers, a corrupt MP tries to take the temple into government custody under the pretense of development. Krishna, an atheist scarred by his mother’s tragic death, supports the MP initially. The temple priest’s daughter — a true devotee of Lord Krishna — opposes the takeover. As the conflict unfolds, Krishna’s past and pain collide with the faith of the village, leading to a powerful emotional climax.

The Silent Shelter

An old man runs a quiet orphanage and old-age home on the outskirts of a city. But he is no ordinary man — he trains the orphans to endure pain without reacting, forging them into emotionally resilient warriors. His mysterious past and the intense methods he uses unfold slowly, revealing that he once stood against a cruel society — and now prepares children to survive it. A story with pain, power, and a deeper truth.
